My Projects

Posters

Poster design uses typography, imagery, and color to create visually compelling messages. It balances aesthetics with clarity to capture attention and convey information effectively, making it a powerful tool for advertising, events, and education.

Illustrations

A uniform stylistic approach enables the composition of a coherent scene or setting, thereby maximising the communication of atmosphere, place, or culture. Wherever incorporated into a design program, animated materials, or instructional aids, these images may be used together to reinforce meaning, introduce complexity, and engage the viewer more effectively than an individual image can achieve.

Artwork

Artwork collaboration in Krita allows artists to create expressive, character-based drawings using powerful digital tools. Krita's brush engines, layer functionality, and vector ability make it the ideal software for the creation of clean line art, rich color, and fine shading, especially for cartoon or animation-themed artworks. The power of working non-destructively with layers and selection allows favourite characters to come alive while giving artists the freedom to test and refine work effortlessly.
